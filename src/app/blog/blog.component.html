T-Res Off-Campus Accomodation

TRes is a digital platform built to enhance the quality of life for students in student accommodation while providing appropriate tools and support to accommodation providers. The design and functionality of the platform aims to create ongoing interaction between students, accommodation providers and University administrators in a spirit of cooperation and mutual respect. Each of the participants must always feel like their voice is heard. Learn more about T-Res here.
